Follow You Printer

To add the follow you printer, please follow the steps below:

  1. Click the Start button.
  2. Select Settings.
  3. Select Devices.
  4. Select Printers & scanners.
  5. Click on the "Add a printer or scanner" button.
  6. Select "The printer that I want isn't listed".
  7. Select "Add a printer using a TCP/IP address or hostname" and click Next.
  8. Enter the following information:

    9. To connect to the follow you printer, please copy and paste the following SMB link into your file explorer or web browser:

    \\sv101-printsvr\follow your printer
